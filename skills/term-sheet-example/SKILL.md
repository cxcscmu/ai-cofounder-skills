## TERM SHEET

**Date:** March 10, 2026

### COMPANY

**Company Name:** Eraser Robotics, Inc.
**Description:** Autonomous robotics company developing whiteboard cleaning systems for schools, offices, and healthcare environments.

### INVESTOR

**Investor Name:** Forge Robotics Ventures, LLC

### FINANCING OVERVIEW

**Round Type:** Pre-Seed (Accelerator + Convertible Note)
**Total Investment:** Up to $150,000
- $75,000 upon execution
- $75,000 upon milestone completion

### EQUITY GRANT

- **Equity Issued:** 3.0%
- **Basis:** Fully diluted (including options, warrants, SAFEs, and convertibles)
- **Timing:** Issued upon entry into Program

### CONVERTIBLE PROMISSORY NOTE

- **Principal Amount:** Up to $150,000
- **Interest Rate:** 6% simple annual interest
- **Maturity Date:** 24 months from issuance
- **Discount Rate:** 20%
- **Valuation Cap:** $6,000,000

### CONVERSION TERMS

The Note converts into equity upon:
- A qualified financing of $1,000,000 or more, or
- At maturity, at the election of the Investor

Conversion price shall be the lower of:
- Price per share in the qualified financing less the 20% discount, or
- Price per share implied by the $6,000,000 valuation cap

### CHANGE OF CONTROL

If the Company undergoes a change of control prior to conversion:
Investor may elect to:
- Convert into equity immediately prior to the transaction, or
- Receive 1.5x the outstanding principal plus accrued interest

### PRO RATA RIGHTS

Investor shall have the right to participate in future equity financings to maintain its ownership percentage.

### INFORMATION RIGHTS

Company shall provide:
- Quarterly financial and operational updates
- Annual financial statements

### USE OF FUNDS

Funds shall be used for:
- Product development and prototyping
- Pilot deployments
- Manufacturing readiness
- Go-to-market activities

### PROGRAM PARTICIPATION

Company agrees to:
- Participate in accelerator programming
- Attend required sessions and Demo Day
- Engage in pilot and commercialization efforts

### MANUFACTURING / LOCATION

Company agrees to maintain a meaningful operational presence in Pennsylvania for at least three (3) years or provide equivalent repayment or compensation.

### MATCHING CONTRIBUTION

Company shall provide matching contributions equal to at least 50% of the total funds received, which may include:
- Cash expenditures
- Founder labor
- Equipment or services

### INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

- All intellectual property shall be assigned to the Company
- All founders, employees, and contractors shall execute invention assignment agreements

### FOUNDER VESTING

- Founder equity subject to four (4) year vesting with a one (1) year cliff
- Unvested shares subject to repurchase

### CONDITIONS PRECEDENT

Prior to funding, the Company must:
- Be incorporated in Delaware or Pennsylvania
- Maintain a clean capitalization table
- Resolve all intellectual property ownership
- Execute all definitive agreements

### PREEMPTIVE RIGHTS

Investor shall have standard preemptive rights to purchase additional securities in future financings. Rights are assignable to affiliates.

### EXPIRATION

This term sheet expires on March 25, 2026, unless extended by the Investor.

### NON-BINDING

This term sheet is non-binding except for provisions relating to confidentiality and exclusivity, if applicable. Definitive agreements will govern the investment.
