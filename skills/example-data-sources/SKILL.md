## 100 Data Sources for Demand Generation

Use this reference list to identify data sources that can power demand generation campaigns, competitive analysis, and customer insights.

### Financial Behavior & Transaction Data

1. **Credit Union NCUA Call Reports** - Quarterly financial performance data from NCUA.gov, member growth rates, loan delinquency trends
2. **Checking Account Transaction Patterns** - Average monthly transactions per member (industry benchmarks from Bankrate, NerdWallet studies)
3. **Loan Application Approval Rates** - Auto loan approval percentages by credit score band (Federal Reserve Consumer Credit reports)
4. **Cross-Sell Ratios** - Products per member metrics from Credit Union Times benchmarking studies
5. **Overdraft Frequency Data** - CFPB overdraft fee reports, average incidents per account
6. **Digital Banking Adoption Rates** - Mobile app login frequency data from Fiserv/FIS digital banking reports
7. **ATM Usage Decline Trends** - Federal Reserve Payments Study showing transaction volume shifts
8. **Person-to-Person Payment Growth** - Zelle, Venmo, CashApp usage statistics from payment network annual reports
9. **Credit Card Balance Transfer Behavior** - TransUnion credit industry insights on balance churn
10. **Auto Loan Refinance Triggers** - Interest rate spread data from Edmunds, Bankrate showing refi opportunities
11. **Home Equity Line Draw Patterns** - Freddie Mac house price index combined with HELOC utilization rates
12. **Certificate of Deposit Maturity Calendars** - FDIC national rate data showing competitive yield gaps
13. **Member Savings Rate Fluctuations** - Bureau of Economic Analysis personal savings rate by region
14. **Bill Pay Adoption Curves** - ACI Worldwide bill payment trend reports
15. **Inactive Account Reactivation Rates** - Industry benchmarks from Aite-Novarica Group studies
